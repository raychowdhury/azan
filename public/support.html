Azan Times Support

Azan Times provides prayer times, weekly schedules, reminders, and Qibla direction.

Location Help

If the app shows the wrong city, open iOS Settings, grant location permission for Azan Times, then tap the location icon again inside the app.

Prayer Time Differences

Prayer times can vary by calculation method. Open Settings in the app and choose the method used by your local mosque or community.

Contact

For support, use the contact email connected to this app's App Store listing.
