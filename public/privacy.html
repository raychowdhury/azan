Azan Times Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Overview

Azan Times is designed to provide prayer times, weekly schedules, reminders, and Qibla direction. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not include analytics SDKs, and does not track users.

Location

When you tap the location button and grant permission, Azan Times uses your device location to calculate local prayer times, show an approximate place name, and point the Qibla compass. Location is used for app functionality and is not sold or used for advertising.

City Search

When you search for a city, that city is used to request prayer time data from the prayer times provider used by the app.

Notifications

If you enable prayer notifications, the app schedules local reminders on your device. These reminders do not require creating an account.

Third-Party Services

Azan Times may access third-party services to provide app features, including Aladhan API for prayer times and Islamic calendar data, and a third-party Adhan audio CDN, currently islamcan.com, for optional Adhan audio playback. Prayer time requests may include a searched city or coordinates needed to calculate prayer times.

Data Stored on Device

The app may store your settings and last selected location on your device so the app can reopen with your previous preferences.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support URL listed on the App Store.
